## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following data when you register or use the site:

* All information you enter during registration (e.g., name, username, email address, etc.)
* Technical session data (session cookies only)

We do **not** use analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party advertising. We also do not process payment information.

## 3. Purpose and Legal Basis

Your data is processed:

* To manage your account and control access (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – performance of a contract)
* To comply with legal obligations if necessary (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR)

## 4. Storage Duration

We retain your data for as long as your account remains active or until you request deletion. Session cookies are stored temporarily and deleted after your session ends.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties unless required by law.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

* Access your personal data
* Rectify incorrect data
* Request deletion of your data
* Restrict or object to data processing
* Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

## 7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.
